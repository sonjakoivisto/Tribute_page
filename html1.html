Lasse Virén

-the runner legend who fell, rose and won-

Finnish runner Lasse Virén (1949- ) is the most known for the legendary 10 000 meter run in 1972 München Olympics. He symbolises the Finnish SISU, loosely translating to strenght or resilience. Virén’s main focus had been on 5 000 meters but in Münich he decided to take part also in 10 000 meters. He fell when colliding with another competitor, Mohammed Gammoud, but got quickly back to his feet and took the leading position. After all, he victoriously gained his first Olympic Gold Medal with a fresh World record 27.38. Virén proved to be as tough on 5 000 meters in Münich, taking the brightest medal with Olympic record.

    VIRÉN IS KNOWN FOR

  • 1972 Gold in Münich Olympics in 10 000 meters (WR) and 5 000 meters (OR)
  • 1972 WR in 5 000 in Helsinki
  • 1976 Gold in Montréal Olympics in 10 000 meters and 5 000 meters
  • 1976 Montréal Olympics was his first marathon, Virén came 5th
  • After his running career Virén has run multiple marathons all around the world
  • Virén broke 3 World Records and 8 Finnish Records during his career
  • Finnish Athlete of the year 1972 and 1976
  • Virén has been active in local politics
  • In 21st century, he has twice been voted for the Parliament
  • Lasse Virén has 2 statues: one in Helsinki around the Olympic stadium and one in his hometown Myrskylä

    VIRÉN ISN’T KNOWN FOR

  • 1949 Virén was born in Myrskylä, Finland. Myrskylä has less than 2 000 inhabitants currently
  • Virén’s father was a truck driver
  • He started training seriously as late as the age of 16, still without a coach
  • 1971 (a year before München) he ran 7 881 km in 722 training sessions
  • 1975 he had a hamstring operation that took away the previous pain in the muscle
  • 1976 Virén ran 25 km in Kaivopuisto, Helsinki with the fastest time ever 1.14.21.
  • 1980 Virén broke his ankle while hunting elk. This could have been the end of his running career but luckily he recovered
  • 1970-1979 Virén worked as a policeman
  • 1992 He took over his father’s logistics company
